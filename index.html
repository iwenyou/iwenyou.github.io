Wheat

Wheat is a on-going web application I am currently working on that allows users to keep track of their groceries. It will track what groceries an user has purchased and will remind them when their groceries are about to expire. It suggests recipes to an user base on what the user's grocery, and allows an user to share and add their recipes to the Wheat community.

For this project, I used Ruby on Rails as my back-end, AngularJS and MDL as my front-end. I will continue working on this project and keep updating it. My final goal is make this application available to the public.
